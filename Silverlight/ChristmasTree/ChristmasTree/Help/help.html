ChristmasTree 1.3.0

Developer: Natalia Derevenets <nna911@inbox.ru>

ChristmasTree is an entertainment application for Windows Phone devices that allows you to decorate your own virtual Christmas Tree.

If you have any questions regarding this application, you can contact application developer on this website: http://christmastree.sourceforge.net/.

How to decorate Christmas Tree

1. Run application and press button to choose how your tree and background image should look like and/or adjust music volume.

2. Press button to open toys gallery.

3. Select toy you like from gallery by holding your finger on it and then drag selected toy to appropriate place on Christmas Tree. Press and hold your finger on the toy to send it to background or bring to foreground. Move toy away from Christmas Tree to remove it.

4. Repeat previous step, choosing different toys from gallery until you get your own wonderfully decorated Christmas Tree. Press button to save image of your Christmas Tree to your phone's gallery.
